# Blooming - Simple Workout Tracking for Telegram

Blooming is a beautiful Telegram Mini App that helps you log workouts, plan your week, and track your fitness journey - all in one place.

## 🌸 What Makes Blooming Special

### Simple & Fast
- Quick workout logging for strength and cardio exercises
- Pre-configured exercise presets (Bench Press, Squat, Treadmill, etc.)
- One-tap workout creation with smart defaults
- No complex forms or unnecessary steps

### Weekly Planner
- Pick your workout days (1-7 per week)
- Choose workout type per day: Upper, Lower, Full Body, or Cardio
- See your entire week at a glance
- Edit plans anytime - changes sync instantly
- Never wonder "what do I do today?"

### Beautiful Themes
- 4 curated themes (2 light, 2 dark)
- Gruvbox Dark (default) - warm, rich earth tones
- Gruvbox Light - perfect for bright environments
- Catppuccin Latte - soft, modern pastel aesthetic
- Dracula - popular dark theme with purple accents
- Switch themes with one tap - your preference is saved

### Your Data, Always There
- Multi-user support with secure profiles
- Works with Supabase cloud backend
- Data syncs across your devices
- Automatic backups - no data loss
- Row-level security keeps your data private

## 🎯 Core Features

### Workout Logging
- **Strength Workouts**: Log sets, reps, weight, and RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion)
- **Cardio Workouts**: Track duration, distance, and RPE
- **Custom Mode**: Override preset values with your own
- **7 Strength Exercises**: Bench Press, Squat, Deadlift, Overhead Press, Pull Up, Cable Fly, Leg Press, Row
- **6 Cardio Exercises**: Treadmill, Cycling, Elliptical, Rowing, Stair Climber, Swimming

### Weekly Scheduler
- Select which days you work out (Monday-Sunday)
- Assign workout types to each day
- Auto-assigns 3 exercises per workout type
- View today's scheduled workout on home screen
- Mark days as completed
- Create, edit, or reset your weekly plan anytime

### Workout History
- View all your past workouts in reverse chronological order
- See exercise details, sets, reps, weight, duration, distance
- Delete individual workouts
- Clear entire history if needed
- Beautiful theme-aware cards

## 🛠️ Built With Modern Technology

### Frontend
- **React 18** - Fast, component-based UI
- **TypeScript** - Type-safe, maintainable code
- **Tailwind CSS** - Utility-first styling with custom themes
- **Vite** - Lightning-fast development and builds

### Backend
- **Supabase** - PostgreSQL database, real-time subscriptions, edge functions
- **Row Level Security** - Users can only access their own data
- **Telegram WebApp SDK** - Native Telegram integration

### Performance
- **389KB** bundle size (113KB gzipped) - Lightning fast loads
- **Code-splitting ready** - Lazy load pages when needed
- **Optimized queries** - Fast database operations with indexes

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

### Mobile-First
- Designed for Telegram Mini App
- Touch-friendly buttons with large tap targets
- Swipe gestures where natural

### Clean & Minimal

## 🚀 How It Works

### For New Users
1. Open Blooming in Telegram
2. Allow Telegram access (one-time)
3. Your profile is created automatically
4. Pick your workout days in the scheduler
5. Start logging workouts - that's it!

### Returning Users
1. Your profile and data load instantly
2. See your workout history immediately
3. Continue your weekly plan
4. All your data is synced and backed up
