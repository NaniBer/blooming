# Blooming

Simple workout tracking for Telegram. Log workouts, plan your week, track your fitness - all in one beautiful app.

- Quick logging for strength & cardio
- Weekly planner with 4 workout types
- 4 beautiful themes (Gruvbox Light/Dark, Catppuccin Latte, Dracula)
- Multi-user support with cloud backup
- Works on iOS, Android, Desktop, Web

Built with React, TypeScript, Supabase. Free forever.
